What is the Fear & Greed Index?

The Crypto Fear & Greed Index measures market emotions on a scale from 0 (Extreme Fear) to 100 (Extreme Greed). It is calculated using volatility, market momentum, social media sentiment, surveys, Bitcoin dominance, and search trends.

Savvy investors often use it as a contrarian signal — when the market is in Extreme Fear, it can signal a potential buying opportunity. When it shows Extreme Greed, the market may be due for a correction.

0–24 Extreme Fear 25–49 Fear 50–74 Greed 75–100 Extreme Greed
